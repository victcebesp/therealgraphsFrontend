Covid-19, commonly known as Coronavirus it is being a worldwide devastating virus. The spread started in China and it has been spread around the world. On the 31st of December of 2019, WHO offices in China received the first report regarding an unknown virus responsible of an increase in pneumonia cases in Wuhan, a city located in the East of China. The first recorded confirmed cases dates from the 22nd of January. Additionally, on the 11th of March the WHO officially declared the Covid-19 outbreak as a pandemic.

A lot of media have been showing different graphs representing Covid-19 data. However, many of them are misleading, confusing and are showing data in absolute terms, not letting people really compare correctly different countries. Because of this, this visualization has been created, plotting the reality of the situation and the fifteen more affected countries in terms of fatal cases with respect to their population.

As displayed in the timer, this dashboard is daily updated with data coming from the GitHub repository of Johns Hopkins University Center for Systems Science and Engineering (JHU CSSE).
